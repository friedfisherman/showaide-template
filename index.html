HOW IT WORKS

Hiring Showing Assistants

Simply insert the details of your client and the date and time requested. Once the system determines there are agents available in the area we will ask you for property addresses and viola you will have a showing assistant setting up showings!

Find out More

Show homes for $$

You can choose to turn on the showing assistant job offers in the show tab. Simply enter a zip code and your desired radius where you will be available. When a job becomes available you will be notified and have the opportunity to accept the showings

Find out More

Open Communication

Both agents will have the ability to call the other agent or the client for any more information or clarification. There is also a text chat feature built in the app.

Find out More

Powerful Dashboard

As a hiring agent you will be able to see what agent is assigned to your job and how far along they are. Have they requested the showing? Has it been approved? Have they completed the showing? As a showing assistant you will be able to see which properties you need to show and what time they need to be scheduled.
